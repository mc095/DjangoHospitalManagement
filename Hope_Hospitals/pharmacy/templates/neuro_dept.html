Neurology Department

Our Neurology department is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for patients with neurological disorders, from diagnosis to treatment and recovery.

We offer a range of services, including diagnosis and management of stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological conditions. Our team of experienced neurologists and neurosurgeons work together to provide personalized care for each patient.

Services features

  • Stroke Diagnosis and Treatment
  • Epilepsy Management and Surgery
  • Multiple Sclerosis Diagnosis and Treatment
  • Neurophysiology and Electromyography
  • Neurosurgery and Interventional Neuroradiology
  • Pain Management and Rehabilitation
