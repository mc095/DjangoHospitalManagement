About Dr. Raj Vikramaditya

Dr. Raj Vikramaditya is a renowned cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating heart-related disorders. He has performed numerous complex cardiac surgeries and has a special interest in minimally invasive cardiac procedures.

Dr. Vikramaditya completed his MBBS from the prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and went on to pursue his MD in Cardiology from the University of Mumbai. He has also received advanced training in cardiac electrophysiology from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA).
