Introducing Dr. Vanshika Singh

Dr. Vanshika Singh is a highly skilled and experienced Neurologist dedicated to providing exceptional care for patients with neurological disorders. With a strong foundation in clinical neurosciences, she has a deep understanding of the complexities of the nervous system and is well-versed in the latest diagnostic and therapeutic techniques.

Her expertise spans a wide range of neurological conditions, including stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and dementia. Dr. Singh is committed to delivering personalized care that addresses the unique needs and concerns of each patient, and she works closely with her patients to develop effective treatment plans that improve their quality of life.
